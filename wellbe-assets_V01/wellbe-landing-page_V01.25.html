Common Questions

What's the story behind WellBe?

WellBe was built by physicians who saw firsthand how little financial guidance exists for doctors in training. We created a private, verified community where physicians can ask real questions, get real answers, and build the financial habits they need — together.

Who can join?

WellBe is a verified physician community — membership is open to all licensed MDs and DOs, residents, and fellows across every specialty.

What can I do inside WellBe?

WellBe is your go-to spot for Good Help Quickly.

  • Learn what repayment or forgiveness path makes sense for your student loans
  • Take the confusion out of securing insurance
  • Set up a simple cash flow system for you and your family
  • See what other physicians are asking (and chime in with your own experience!)
  • Get Real, Helpful Answers — fast

Will it take up a lot of my time?

As little or as much as you want. Most members drop in for a few minutes a day. Everything is asynchronous, and live events are always recorded, so you can have access to these resources and our experts whenever you need them.
